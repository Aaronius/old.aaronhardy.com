On Oct. 24, we raked leaves for several elderly people in our community.  They were very appreciative, and we had seven members attend.

 

On Dec. 16, our club members and additional students went around town to many homes singing Christmas songs.  Not only did we serve the community with our songs, we also had a great time.  Twelve members attended.

 

On Jan. 9, we cleaned up garbage that was along a section of our highway.  There were six members helping.

 

Our chapter has helped clean up the gymnasium after several of our home athletic events, and most members have participated throughout the season.

 

Later this spring, we will be helping our elementary school with their "Reading is Fundamental" book program. This will include setting up displays and assisting the supervisors. Our members have all expressed an interest in this and are planning to help.
