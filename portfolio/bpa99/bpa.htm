Business Professionals of America is a student organization that aims to prepare future business professionals through competitions in citizenship, leadership, academic, and technological skills.

 

There are two divisions of BPA: Secondary and Post-Secondary.  Students in high school and college are eligible to register with their school chapters and become involved in the activities and competitions.

 

BPA is a fun and exciting way to learn more about business and leadership.   Members have a great time participating in these social activities.

 

To see if your school is involved, contact your school administrators.  If your school does not have a BPA chapter, click here for the official BPA website or fill out this form for more information.
