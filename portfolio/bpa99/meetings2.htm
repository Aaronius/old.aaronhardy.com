On Sept. 22, we held Inductions for our club members.  We reviewed the objectives of BPA, and each member swore to the following: I, ______, as a member of the OHS Chapter of Business Professionals of America, do solemnly swear that I will strive for excellence and aim for higher than perfection in my business pursuits.  Only one member was absent, and he was inducted later.

 

On Sept. 22, we also held an election for our chapter officers.  We had open-nominations and then voted for our president, vice-president, and secretary.  For more information, go to our Chapter Officers page.

 

Seven of our members attended a leadership workshop on Nov.12-14 in Boise.  This was a great experience and provided our chapter with useful ideas for fund-raisers and social activities.

 

On Dec. 7, we had a meeting to register for our regional events, and all members attended.  For more information about our chapter members and their events, go to our Members page.

 

On Jan. 11, we held a meeting to review our events and prepare for regional competition.  All members attended.

 

On Jan. 11, we also had Kristen Hardy give a presentation to our members about the National Leadership Conference she attended last spring.
