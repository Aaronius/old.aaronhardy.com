Our club has been selling several types of jerky at school since the beginning of the year.  This has been profitable to our club because the jerky is a popular item at our school.

 

On Nov. 14, we sponsored a school dance after a basketball game.  This was a fun and profitable event, and of course all members attended.

 

We have held a couple of potato bars at our school to help us pay our fees.  These are very successful at our school, and all of our members have participated.  These were held on Nov. 25 and Dec. 11.

 

On Dec. 10, we held a hoop shoot during half-time of a basketball game.  Eleven of our members attended the game, but only three were needed to help.

 

Our school has a student store which they allow us to use for club fund-raising.  We use this store during our lunch periods to help pay for our state competition fees, and all members contribute their time.

 

On Feb. 11, we had an airplane throw contest during half-time of a basketball game.  The participants threw paper airplanes onto the court for prizes, and they all had a great time.  All members attended, but only five were needed.
