OLD CONTEST OUTLINES
 

As of right now (December 5, 1999), my car's odometer is sitting at 199,257 miles. The question is: at what point in time will it roll over to 200,000 miles? My car is a 1985 Honda Accord. Although it may seem old, I love it dearly - at least the motor anyway. Everything else isn't so top-notch. To explain:
1) I should just make plans to cut the top off and make it a convertable. Afterall, it wouldn't make much difference in air flow or temperature inside the car.
2) Anyone want a full-body massage? Go ahead and sit on my dashboard.
3) Anyone for some target practice? I don't mind if you shoot the side of my car, afterall it already looks like somebody's taken a shot-gun to it. (This is do to door-dings. There are no dents due to poor driving, keep in mind.)
Now, since you've got most of the details of my car I'll explain the rules, etc: put in your name, email address, address, and the time you think it will roll over to 200,000 miles and send it off. You can only vote once per day and the closest guesser (guessee, guessist?) will get a prize not yet decided on, but I assure you it will be well worth the effort. Remember, you can vote once per day so keep trying as often as possible for a better chance of winning. The information you provide will in no way be sold or distributed and you won't be annoyed by any ads or anything else. It only be used to notify you when I hit 200K. On to the contest...
